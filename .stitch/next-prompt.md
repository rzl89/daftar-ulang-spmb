Design a premium, modern school registration homepage for SMKN 5 Kota Serang (a vocational high school in Indonesia). The page is for 'Daftar Ulang' (re-registration) of new students.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Primary color: Navy Blue #1A237E
- Accent color: Gold #F9A825 
- Font: Poppins/Inter
- Style: Clean, professional, government/education feel
- Rounded corners (16px)
- Background: Light gray #f8fafc

**Page Structure:**
1. STICKY NAVBAR - Logo with graduation cap icon + 'SMKN 5 KOTA SERANG' text + 'SPMB 2025/2026' subtitle. Navigation links: Beranda, Daftar Ulang, Cek Status, Informasi, Kontak. Right side: 'Cek Status' outline button + 'Daftar Ulang' gold CTA button with pulse glow effect.

2. HERO SECTION - Full-width navy gradient background (135deg, #1A237E to #0D1257). Gold badge 'SPMB Tahun Ajaran 2025/2026' with award icon. Large bold white title 'Daftar Ulang' and gold text 'Penerimaan Murid Baru'. Subtitle text in white/60% opacity. Countdown timer showing Days and Hours in glassmorphism cards with gold numbers. Two CTA buttons: gold 'Mulai Daftar Ulang' with sparkle icon and semi-transparent 'Cek Status Pendaftaran' with search icon. Decorative subtle circles and blur effects. Wave SVG at bottom transitioning to light section.

3. QUICK ACTIONS GRID - 4 cards in a row: Daftar Ulang (navy icon), Cek Status (gold icon), Informasi (green icon), Hubungi Kami (navy-light icon). Each card has colored icon box, bold title, short description. Hover: shadow + slight lift.

4. REGISTRATION FLOW - Section title 'Alur Pendaftaran Ulang' with gold badge 'PANDUAN LANGKAH'. 4 step cards: Baca Persyaratan, Isi Formulir, Upload Dokumen, Verifikasi. Each with step number watermark, gold icon, step badge, title, description.

5. JURUSAN SECTION - White background. Title 'Pilihan Jurusan' with navy badge. 6 cards in 3-column grid for: TJKT, PPLG, DKV, AKL, MP, BDP. Each card shows code in navy box (hover: navy bg with gold text) + full name + 'Program Keahlian' label.

6. BOTTOM CTA - Navy gradient card with decorative blurs. Gold badge 'BATAS WAKTU'. Title 'Jangan Lewatkan Batas Waktu Pendaftaran!' with gold accent. Gold CTA button 'Daftar Ulang Sekarang'.

7. FOOTER - Navy gradient. 4-column layout: Branding (logo, tagline 'Disiplin, Cerdas dan Terampil'), Navigation links, Contact info (address, phone, email, website), Hours + Social media icons. Bottom bar with copyright.

Make it look extremely polished, premium, and professional - like a top-tier government education portal.
